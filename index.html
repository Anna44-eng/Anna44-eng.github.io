Brownie Recipie

Ingredients

  • 185g dark chocolate
  • 185g butter
  • 275 g dark brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 80g plain flour
  • 40g cocoa powder

Steps

  • Melt the chocolate, once melted, leave to cool.
  • Wisk the sugar and butter together. Once combined, add 3 eggs and whisk.
  • Once the chocolate has cooled, add it into the sugar mixture and fold.
  • Add your flour and fold carefully
  • Here you can add any additions, I like to use chocolate chunks and sometimes peanut butter.
  • Pour you mixture into a lined square tin and leave in the oven at 180 degreed for 20 minutes.
  • Let you brownies cool and enjoy the best browines you will ever make!

Author: Anna Gill
